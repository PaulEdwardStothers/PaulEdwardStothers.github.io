biography

Paul Edward Stothers (b. 1975, Wairoa, New Zealand) is an interdisciplinary artist who lives and works in Tāmaki Makaurau, Auckland. His practice encompasses video, sound, costume, fashion and mixed media — often found outside a gallery context. Stothers' work playfully recontextualises found media, emergent or obsolete internet technologies and popular culture.
